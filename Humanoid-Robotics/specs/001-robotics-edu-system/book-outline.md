# Book Outline: Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Table of Contents

### Chapter Naming Convention:
*   `ChapterN_Title.mdx` (e.g., `Chapter01_Introduction.mdx`)

### General Learning Objectives per Chapter:
*   **Understand:** Key concepts, theories, and principles.
*   **Identify:** Different components, types, or methods.
*   **Analyze:** Performance, trade-offs, and implications.
*   **Apply:** Techniques, algorithms, or tools to solve problems.
*   **Evaluate:** Different approaches or solutions.
